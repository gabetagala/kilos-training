PRIVACY

Last updated: 31 May 2026

KILOS Training ("KILOS", "we") is a free workout tracker. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the control you have. Plain language, no tricks.

The short version

KILOS works on your device first. Your workouts, body weight, personal records and settings are saved in your browser's local storage and never leave your phone unless you choose to create an account to sync. We don't sell your data, we don't run ad trackers, and you can export or delete everything at any time.

What we store on your device

Your logged workouts and sets, body weight and personal records, streaks, and your profile (your name, chosen equipment, and kg/lbs preference). This stays on your device and is yours to export (CSV) or clear at any time.

What we store in the cloud — only if you create an account

An account is optional and exists only to sync across your devices. If you create one we store, via our processor Supabase:

  • A username and password (we use a synthetic email derived from your username; we do not require or collect a real email address).
  • A copy of your training data (the same workouts, weights, PRs and settings described above) and the time it last synced.

Access is protected by per-user row-level security: only your signed-in account can read or write your row.

Diagnostics

To keep the app reliable we may use an error-monitoring service (Sentry) that records crash diagnostics (e.g. error messages and the screen where a crash happened) so we can fix bugs. We configure it to avoid collecting personal information. We do not use advertising or analytics tracking SDKs.

Feedback

If you send a message through the in-app feedback button, its contents are delivered to us by the form service Formspree so we can read and reply.

Your rights

You can, at any time: access and export your data (CSV export on the Home screen), correct it by editing in the app, and delete it — clearing local data on your device, and deleting your account (which removes your synced data) from your profile. Under the Philippines Data Privacy Act of 2012 you also have the right to object, to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission.

Data retention

Local data stays until you clear it or uninstall. Cloud data stays until you delete your account, after which your data row is removed.

Children

KILOS is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect their data.

Contact

Questions or requests about your data: gabe@kilostraining.app. We're a small team and read every message.

This policy may be updated as the app grows; the date above reflects the latest version.
